Lunges using Dumbbells

  • Stand upright with dumbbells at your side
  • Lunge forward as far as you can with your right leg
  • Use the heel of your right foot to push your upper body
  • Repeat with the opposite leg

Dead lifts

  • Position the feet shoulder-width apart
  • Squat down, bending at the knees with back straight or arched in slightly
  • Grasp the bar just outside the line of the knees
  • Lift the bar by pushing upward with the legs from the knees.
  • The bar should almost graze the shins and come to rest around thigh level as you reach full height
  • Lower the bar to the floor with a reverse motion
  • Repeat for your desired number of repetitions

Seated Leg Extension

  • Set up the leg extension
  • Select a weight that will give you a moderate load
  • Place your hands on the hand bars.
  • Lift the weight while exhaling until your legs are almost straight
  • Exhale and lower the weight back to starting position

Seated Legs Curl

  • Set up the machine so that the pads sit comfortably
  • Grasp the side handles on the machine
  • Lift your legs so they are straight out in front
  • Pull the padded lever down and back towards you as far as you can with your legs
  • hold for a second, then slowly bring it back
