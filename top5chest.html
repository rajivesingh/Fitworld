Best Exercises For A Bigger Chest

Bench-Press

  • Lie down on your back on a flat bench
  • Place your legs on side of the bench
  • Grab the bar with both hands with a wide angle
  • Take a deep breath. Draw the bar down until it touches your chest
  • Breath out as you take the bar away from your chest
  • repeat 8-10 times

Incline Bench-Press

  • Set up your bench at an angle of around 30-45°.
  • Lie back and place your hands on the bar slightly wider than shoulder
  • Lift the bar out of the rack and press it up until your arms are extended
  • Slowly lower the bar to your chest, then press it back up again.
  • repeat 10-12 times

Dumbbell Bench Press

  • Set the incline to between 30- to 45-degrees
  • Sit on the bench and lean back
  • Press both dumbbells straight up over your chest as you exhale
  • Slowly lower the dumbbells back to the top of your chest as you inhale
  • repeat 10-12 times

Dumbbell Flyes

  • Sit down on a flat bench with a dumbbell in each hand
  • Lower yourself down until you’re lying flat.
  • Once you’re lying flat, press the dumbbells up above your chest.
  • lower your arms laterally until you feel a stretch in your chest.
  • Return your arms to the starting position and squeeze
  • Don’t let the dumbbells touch as they meet at the top
  • Repeat 10-12 times

Push-up

  • Get on the floor on all fours
  • Positioning your hands slightly wider than your shoulders
  • Inhale as you slowly bend your elbows and lower yourself to the floor
  • Exhale while contracting your chest muscles and pushing back up through your hands
